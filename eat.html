Food, glorious food!

Nodaiwa

This place has the best unagi in all of Tokyo. They have a couple of locations, however I prefer the location in the Ginza train station, which happens to be across the hallway from 3 Michelin Star restaurant, Jiro.

Nodaiwa

Tsukamoto Bldg., fl. B1

4-2-15 Ginza Chuo-ku, 104-0061

Tempura Motoyoshi

This Michelin star tempura is absolutely worth the splurge. Reservations go quickly so book before you go to Tokyo. Your hotel concierge can call for you. They are not on OpenTable.

This is not a picture of the tempura served there. No cameras allowed so this photo is a placeholder. Doesn't do the real thing justice.

Tempura Motoyoshi

3 Chome-2-4

Minamiaoyama, Minato City 107-0062

Henry's Burgers

These burgers are better than Shake Shack. There, I said it. This location is in Daikanyama, not far from the Tsutaya Book Shop. There are only a few seats at the counter, so there is always a line. It moves quickly, but this burger is worth it.

Henry's Burgers

1 Chome-36-6 Ebisunishi

Shibuya City, Tokyo 150-0021

Mitsukoshi Food Hall - Ginza

The major department stores in Tokyo have food halls on lower floors. It is a food lovers paradise. You can get deliciously prepared meals, from sushi, tempura, grilled meats and the most desserts that rival french patisseries. Do yourself a favor and skip dinner in a restaurant enjoy the most incredible meal at a food hall. You won't regret it.

Mitsukoshi Ginza

4 Chome-6-1-6

Ginza, Chuo City 104-8212
